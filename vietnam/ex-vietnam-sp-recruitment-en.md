## 5. Vietnam Market Opportunity

### 5.1 China-Vietnam Trade Scenario

- A large number of merchants under EX Clients **export to Vietnam** (raw materials, equipment, consumer goods)
- Strong demand for local collection and Vietnamese Dong (VND) settlement in Vietnam
- Chinese merchants need Vietnam local VA collection + VND→USD/CNY FX

### 5.2 Vietnam Export to Europe/US Scenario

- Vietnam's manufacturing exports to Europe and the US continue to grow (textiles, electronics, furniture)
- Vietnamese exporters need **efficient, low-cost cross-border collection solutions**
- EX Clients can serve these Vietnamese exporters but currently lack local Vietnam service provider capabilities

### 5.3 Vietnam Local E-Commerce Scenario

- Local Vietnamese e-commerce sellers need instant VND settlement
- Cross-border platform collections need to be settled through local clearing

### 5.4 Market Background Reference

| Dimension | Overview |
|-----------|----------|
| **Vietnam Cross-Border E-Commerce** | Sustained high growth; one of the most dynamic markets in Southeast Asia |
| **China-Vietnam Trade** | Bilateral trade volume continues to rise; strong cross-border payment demand |
| **Vietnam Exports** | Large-scale manufacturing exports to Europe/US; clear demand for cross-border settlement |
| **Local Service Providers** | Scarce supply of quality licensed PSPs in Vietnam; insufficient channel availability |

## 8. Proprietary AI Smart Risk Control System

EurewaX's independently developed AI smart risk control engine is the platform's core moat, **safeguarding service provider operations**.

### 8.1 Six Risk Control Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Merchant Onboarding KYC/KYB** | AI-powered document recognition, UBO penetration, director/legal representative verification, adverse list screening |
| **Transaction Anti-Fraud** | Dual-layer decision-making via rule engine and model scoring; identifies abnormal transactions, device fingerprinting, IP risk, behavioral anomalies |
| **AML / CFT Monitoring** | Continuous transaction monitoring, affiliation analysis, automated Suspicious Transaction Report (STR) generation |
| **On-Chain Risk Control** | Stablecoin address profiling, on-chain traceability, mixer identification, sanctioned address screening |
| **Configurable Rules** | Risk control strategies support flexible configuration by client, product, region, and currency dimensions |
| **Regulatory Reporting** | Automated compliance report generation, regulatory audit trail, KYC/KYB document archiving |

### 8.2 Full-Link Risk Control Process

```
Onboarding Review → Transaction Pre-check → Real-time Monitoring → Post-hoc Analysis → Regulatory Reporting
   KYC/KYB           Risk Scoring          AML / Anti-Fraud      Affiliation / Suspicious   Compliance Trail
```

### 8.3 Flexible Delivery Methods

Risk control capabilities support two delivery forms: **modular split invocation** and **full-link unified service**:

| Delivery Form | Applicable Scenarios |
|---------------|----------------------|
| **Single-Point Service** | On-demand invocation of a single risk control capability (e.g., KYB review only, AML list screening only, on-chain address verification only) |
| **Combined Service** | Invocation of multiple capabilities in combination (e.g., KYC + Anti-Fraud + AML), adapting to differentiated risk control needs across product lines |
| **Full-Link Unified Service** | One-stop access from merchant onboarding to transaction monitoring, post-hoc analysis to regulatory reporting |
