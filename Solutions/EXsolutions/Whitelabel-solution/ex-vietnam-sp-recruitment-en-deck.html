Vietnam Market Opportunity · Market Opportunity

Three Business Scenarios + Market Background
A China-Vietnam Trade
  • A large number of merchants under EX Clients export to Vietnam (raw materials, equipment, consumer goods)
  • Strong demand for local collection and VND settlement in Vietnam
  • Chinese merchants need Vietnam local VA + VND→USD/CNY FX
B Vietnam Exports to EU/US
  • Vietnam's manufacturing exports to Europe and the US continue to grow (textiles, electronics, furniture)
  • Vietnamese exporters need efficient, low-cost cross-border collection solutions
  • EX Clients can serve these Vietnamese exporters but currently lack local Vietnam SP capabilities
C Vietnam Local E-Commerce
  • Local Vietnamese e-commerce sellers need instant VND settlement
  • Cross-border platform collections need to be settled through local clearing
  • One of the most dynamic e-commerce markets in Southeast Asia, with sustained high growth

Commercial Return Model · Revenue Model

Four Types of Revenue Streams for Service Providers
💵
Transaction Fee Income

Fees charged based on transaction volume; service providers set their own pricing. Vietnam local clearing fees and cross-border payout service fees are all led by service providers.

🔁
FX Spread

Participation in VND ↔ USD / CNY and other multi-currency exchange businesses, earning exchange rate spreads as a stable non-transactional revenue stream.

📊
Scale Effect

As EX Clients and merchant volumes grow, service provider transaction volumes naturally scale, without additional BD investment.

🌐
Ecosystem Binding

EX Clients are distributed across multiple verticals (e-commerce / Web3 / foreign trade / card issuance), with diversified transaction types and strong risk resilience.
