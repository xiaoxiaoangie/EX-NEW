1Solution Overview

EX provides a White Label Portal solution — out of the box, no development required.

The Web3 White Label solution focuses on three product pillars: Crypto On/Off Ramp, Card Issuing, and Crypto Checkout, targeting crypto exchanges, digital asset service providers, and Web3 payment platforms.

Based on the product lines activated by the customer, the EX operations team completes configuration and delivers two portals:

Portal Abbreviation User Description
Tenant Portal TP Customer (Institution) The operations management console used by the customer to manage merchants, view transactions, configure products, etc.
Merchant Portal MP Customer's end users (Merchants) The operations portal delivered to end merchants — merchants self-serve crypto exchange, card issuing, checkout, etc.

Core Value: Customers can launch their Web3 business quickly with zero development investment — once products are activated, they immediately get a complete operations console (TP) and a merchant-facing portal (MP).

4.1 TP (Tenant Portal) — For the Customer

Module Description Notes
Merchant Management Register merchants, submit & track basic information, manage merchant status
Merchant Product Inquiry View products activated by merchants. Adding additional products is planned for June; currently only one product per merchant. If a customer activates one product and then wants another, the operations team's support is required.
Merchant Fee Management Manage merchant product fees Standard pricing must be emailed to operations. Merchant-specific pricing is configurable here. Requires one configurator and one approver.
Merchant FX Pricing Coming Soon Manage merchant ramp exchange rate markup Default unified markup via operations; merchant-specific configurations set here.
Merchant Transactions View all merchants' ramp transactions, card transactions, checkout transactions Displayed by product line
Ticket Approval Approvers for fees and rates review tickets — only effective after approval
My Commission Your transaction commission bills and settlement statements All products
Add-on: Sales Management Generate a customer-acquisition link for each salesperson
Add-on: Sub-Agency Management Develop sub-agencies at the next tier Currently only transactions support rebates; FX rebates not yet supported. Operations manual available from the operations team.
Account Permissions Assign accounts to other users Permissions currently only control page access, not actions.

Module Description Notes
Merchant Registration & Onboarding Submit materials required for ramp/card issuing/checkout activation, including KYC and business information

6Delivery Process

├── 1. Offline Contract Signing
│     └── Sign contract with the account manager
│
├── 2. EX Operations Configuration (1-2 business days)
│     ├── Create tenant
│     ├── Configure branding (logo, domain, email templates)
│     └── Configure standard merchant pricing / FX rates
│
├── 3. Deliver TP (Tenant Portal)
│     └── Customer gets access to the TP console
│     └── Customer can start registering and managing merchants in TP
│
├── 4. Deliver MP (Merchant Portal)
│     └── Merchants register via the tenant link
│     └── Complete KYC/KYB → Activate products → Start using
│
└── 5. Go Live
      └── Customer manages daily operations via TP
      └── End merchants self-serve via MP
