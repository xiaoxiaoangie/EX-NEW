1Solution Overview

Based on the product lines activated by the customer, the EX operations team completes the configuration and delivers two portals:

Portal Abbreviation User Description
Tenant Portal TP Customer (Institution) The operations management console used by the customer to manage merchants, view transactions, configure products, etc.
Merchant Portal MP Customer's end users (Merchants) The operations portal that the customer delivers to its end merchants — merchants self-serve collection, payout, card issuing, etc.

Core Value: Customers can launch their business quickly with zero development investment — once products are activated, they immediately get a complete operations console (TP) and a merchant-facing portal (MP).

2Who Is White Label For

Customer Type Scenario Why Recommend
Agency / Reseller Reselling EX products to end merchants No development; manage merchants via TP, deliver MP to merchants
Cross-border E-commerce SaaS Provide collection & payout services to sellers Quick go-live; sellers operate directly in MP
Early-stage Fintech Just starting, prioritize validating the business model Zero dev cost; validate business first, then consider API integration
Traditional Trading Company Need digital collection/payout tools but no tech team Out-of-the-box portals lower the digitalization barrier
Licensed Financial Institution Expand new business lines (e.g., card issuing, crypto) TP-managed; rapidly expand product lines

3White Label Capability Matrix

Based on the product lines activated by the customer, TP and MP automatically display the corresponding modules:

TP (Tenant Portal) — For the Customer

Module Description Notes
Merchant Management Register merchants, submit & track basic information, manage merchant status
Merchant Product Inquiry View products activated by merchants. Adding additional products is planned for June; currently only one product per merchant is supported. Per SP requirements, business is currently split into B2B and B2C (Shopee, TikTok, Amazon, etc.). If a customer activates a B2B business and then wants to add a B2C business, the operations team's support is required.
Merchant Fee Management Manage merchant product fees Standard pricing for customers must be emailed to operations. If a merchant requires custom pricing, individual fee configuration is supported. Requires one configurator and one approver.
Merchant FX Pricing Manage merchant FX markup Same as above — default unified markup is configured via operations; merchant-specific configurations are set here.
Merchant Transactions View all merchants' collections, payouts, FX Displayed by product line
Trade Background Materials Merchant trade orders, store management
Ticket Approval Approvers for fees and FX rates approve tickets — only effective after approval
My Commission Your transaction commission bills and settlement statements All products
Add-on: Sales Management Generate a customer-acquisition link for each salesperson
Add-on: Sub-Agency Management Develop sub-agencies at the next tier Currently only transactions support rebates; FX rebates are not yet supported. Operations manual available from the operations team.
Account Permissions Assign accounts to other users Permissions currently only control page access, not actions.

MP (Merchant Portal) — For End Merchants

Module Description Notes
Merchant Registration & Onboarding Activate products (e.g., trade collection); submit required materials including KYC and business information Subject to what the SP supports
Fiat Collection Apply for VA, view collection records, download receipts Distinguishes B2B trade collection from B2C e-commerce collection
Fiat Payout Add payees, initiate payouts, settle to RMB, view payout records Add payee first, then initiate payout
FX Conversion Query rates, initiate FX conversion Currently real-time FX product
Trade Documents Upload / manage trade background documents Fiat product
Store Management Manage your e-commerce platform stores, etc.

4Delivery Process

├── 1. Offline Contract Signing
│     └── Sign contract with the account manager
│
├── 2. EX Operations Configuration (1-2 business days)
│     ├── Create tenant
│     ├── Configure branding (logo, domain, email templates)
│     └── Configure standard merchant pricing / FX rates
│
├── 3. Deliver TP (Tenant Portal)
│     └── Customer gets access to the TP console
│     └── Customer can start registering and managing merchants in TP
│
├── 4. Deliver MP (Merchant Portal)
│     └── Merchants register via the tenant link
│     └── Complete KYC/KYB → Activate products → Start using
│
└── 5. Go Live
      └── Customer manages daily operations via TP
      └── End merchants self-serve via MP
